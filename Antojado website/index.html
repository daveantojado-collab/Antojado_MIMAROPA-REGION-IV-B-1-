MIMAROPA Tourism

Explore the Hidden Gems of Region IV-B

MIMAROPA, or Region IV-B, is a stunning archipelago in the Philippines comprising Mindoro, Marinduque, Romblon, and Palawan. Known for its pristine beaches, limestone cliffs, and rich biodiversity, it's a paradise for adventurers and nature lovers. Discover world-class diving spots, vibrant cultures, and untouched landscapes that make this region a must-visit destination.
